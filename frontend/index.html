How It Works

Three steps to study smarter.

1. Understand

Paste notes, slides, or textbook pages. StudentU explains concepts in simple language without assuming prior knowledge.

2. Practice

Generate quizzes from your class materials. Not generic questions, but ones built from what you actually need to learn.

3. Remember

StudentU tracks concepts you struggle with and automatically brings them back before you forget them.
