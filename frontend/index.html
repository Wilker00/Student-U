FAQ

Questions before you upload

Your notes stay scoped to your study space. Here is how StudentU handles privacy, verification, and academic use.

Is my class material private?

Yes. Materials are tied to your study space, not shared publicly. You can export or remove class data anytime from Account Settings.

What does .edu verification unlock?

Verified students get expanded study access, more AI-generated quizzes, and smarter spaced-review planning. You can still try the demo without verifying.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

StudentU remembers your class context, tracks weak topics, and builds a study plan from your syllabus and notes — not one-off chat sessions.

Can I use StudentU for exams and homework?

StudentU is built for learning and review — understanding concepts, practicing recall, and planning study time. Follow your school's academic integrity policies for graded work.
